Nestled on eighty acres of pristine Canadian forest, Balsam Falls is a beautiful riverside retreat teeming with vibrant bio-diversity and rich history. Everywhere you look, you’ll find unique features and thoughtful touches that’ll make your stay truly exceptional.

Surrounded by forest, powered by the sun. A slice of northern paradise.

"The views from the cottage were spectacular. So peaceful. You’re surrounded by nature. The only thing you can hear are the birds and the waterfall."

The house unfolds with an open concept and cathedral ceilings, where large windows frame sweeping views of the river and forest. Elegant dining areas, a gourmet kitchen, and a cozy downstairs theatre invite continual relaxation.

Three boutique-style bedrooms blend comfort and charm, with a daybed offering extra slumber. The two full bathrooms are stocked luxurious bio-degradable amenities.
