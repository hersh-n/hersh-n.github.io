Total nature immersion, for meaningful gatherings, and intentional living.

Luxury Riverside Rental in Bancroft, Ontario

2.5 hours north-east from the GTA, nestled on eighty acres of pristine Canadian forest, Balsam Falls is a beautiful riverside retreat teeming with vibrant bio-diversity and rich history.

When our family first stumbled across this land, we knew we’d found something special. Now, we want to share this vision and space with you.

Surrounded by forest, powered by sun. A slice of northern paradise.

More then a holiday rental, Balsam Falls is an experience. Everywhere you look, you’ll find unique features and thoughtful touches that’ll make your stay truly exceptional.
